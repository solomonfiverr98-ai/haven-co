# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Brand Personality
* Sophisticated and trustworthy — not salesy
* Warm and personal — not corporate
* Aspirational — help clients imagine their perfect home
* Expert and authoritative — backed by results
* Premium — this is a luxury real estate brand

## Grammar & Mechanics
* **Headlines:** Elegant and aspirational. Use serif typography energy in copy too.
* **Body copy:** Warm, personal, benefit-focused. Speak to emotions not features.
* **CTAs:** Inviting not pushy. "Find Your Home" not "Submit Now"
* **Avoid:** Aggressive sales language, clichés like "dream home", generic real estate speak
* **Use:** Specific numbers, real results, emotional language about home and belonging

## Market Context
* This is a UNIVERSAL template — no specific city or country references
* Use $ USD as default currency
* Property prices should feel premium but realistic
* Copy works for any real estate market worldwide

## Terminology Guide

| Do Not Use | Use Instead |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Dream home" | "Perfect home" or "Your ideal property" |
| "Best deal" | "Exceptional value" |
| "Submit enquiry" | "Start your search" |
| "Check out our listings" | "Explore our properties" |
| "We sell houses" | "We find the right home for you" |
| Any specific city name | Remove or use generic reference |

## Copy Examples by Section

**Hero headline style:**
"Find Your Perfect Home." — simple, direct, aspirational

**Property card style:**
"4 Bed · 3 Bath · 2,400 sqft · $1,250,000"
Numbers first, clean and scannable

**Testimonial style:**
"Haven & Co found us our forever home in just 3 weeks. 
The process was seamless from start to finish."
— Outcome first, then emotion

**CTA style:**
"Get Your Free Property Valuation →" — specific value, clear benefit
